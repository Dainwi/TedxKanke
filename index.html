About TEDxKanke

TEDxKanke brings together visionaries and change-makers to share their ideas and inspire action in our community and beyond.

  • Diverse Speakers

    Our lineup features a diverse range of speakers, each with unique perspectives and powerful ideas.

  • Engaging Discussions

    Join us for thought-provoking discussions and interactive sessions that will spark new insights and connections.

  • Community Impact

    TEDxKanke is committed to making a positive impact in our community by sharing ideas and fostering collaboration.
